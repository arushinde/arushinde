About Arush Shinde

Arush Suresh Shinde is sixteen years old. He had just cleared his SSC Board Examination. He completed his school education i.e. till 10th class in Abhinava Vidyalaya High School.

Now, In 11th standanrd Arush is going to choose Science stream because he want to become Web developer. Also he started learning web development, app development and programming languages needed for it. And he made some simple websites and android applications. Other than this, his hobbies are photography, photo editing, audio mixing, writing, etc. Arush have interest in poetry as well. Excepting this, He love to play Tabla.


Early life

Arush was born on 24 October 2004 in Pune, Maharashtra. He has a sister who is two years older than him.

He attended Abhinava Vidyalaya Primary School and then went to Abhinava Vidyalaya High School from 2015 to 2020. He studied at Abhinava Vidyalaya, Erandawne, from 2007 to 2020, where he received many prizes in playing tabla. When he was 5 years old, started learning tabla.
